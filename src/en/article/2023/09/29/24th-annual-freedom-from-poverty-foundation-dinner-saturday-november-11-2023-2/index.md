The 24th Annual Freedom from Poverty Foundation Fundraising Dinner is
happening:

As you are aware that Freedom from Poverty Inc. has been in existence since
1993 and will be organizing its 2th annual gala dinner on Saturday November
11,2023 at Le Chateaubriand. We request you to please reserve that date and
would love to see you at our fundraising dinner.

Thanks to your very generous donations the Foundation was able to support 5
surgical camps and numerous clinics -that were held in 2023- and we were able
to treat over 1000 patients.

With our annual foundation gala occurring in November 2023, we are aiming to
expand our outreach socially. Important to note that from now on, please use
this email to contact the foundation for any related questions.

We are looking forward to keeping you updated more regularly about our efforts
through email blasts and social media platforms.

Saturday, 11 November 2023, at  
Le Chateaubriand, 7985 Blvd Maurice-Duplessis, Montreal,QC H1E 1M5
